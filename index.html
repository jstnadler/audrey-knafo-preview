Home page — three directions

Three distinct treatments of the home page, all built with Audrey's own photographs and a working FR/EN toggle. Click any to open the full prototype. She picks one (or a hybrid) — then we build it as a real Shopify theme.

Direction A

Cinematic Hero

Full-bleed cross-fading hero on a dark, moody palette built around her dramatic B&W work. Most artistic; print store sits below the fold.
Direction B · recommended

Editorial Split

50/50 name + statement vs. featured portrait on warm paper. Treats portfolio and print store as equals above the fold.
Direction C

Series-First Grid

Lands directly on all six series in a gallery grid. Most navigable; quickest path into the work.
Two findings worth Audrey's input (from pulling her live site):
  • Her work is mostly dramatic black-and-white (Portrait, Stand Up, Backstage, Live Show), with color concentrated in Fashion (glamour) and Awards. The three palettes here are tuned for that — but it's a real correction to our earlier "color photography" note.
  • "Awards" is actually award-winning travel / landscape work (horses on a beach, boats, cityscapes) — a different genre from the rest. Worth deciding whether it belongs on the home grid or gets its own home.
