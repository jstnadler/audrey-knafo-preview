What's different from v1
  • One site, not two. Forget runs portfolio + shop on two domains; we're combining them on one Shopify site.
  • Shop IA by edition tier instead of by series. Three collections: Open Edition / Edition 30 / Edition 10. (Per-edition caps still TBD with Audrey.)
  • Product page = split layout (image left, buy right with dropdowns), matching Forget. Sections below: About the piece / Location / Size guide & editions / Shipping / About the artist.
  • Aesthetic shift — white background, uppercase thin sans, no warm-paper or gold accent. Closer to Forget's neutral gallery feel. Final palette decision still open with client.
  • Series pages still exist as portfolio surfaces (her work — Portrait, Stand Up, etc.) but they're separate from the Editions shop.
